Process

How we work together

A clear, collaborative process from first contact to final delivery.

01

Brief & Scope

We discuss your project — what needs to be visualized, the purpose of the images, timelines, and budget. I ask the right questions so nothing is missed.

02

Concept & Camera

I propose camera angles, lighting mood, and a general direction. You review and refine — this is where we align on vision before production begins.

03

Production

3D modelling, lighting, materials, and rendering. You receive preview drafts at key stages so feedback is incorporated early — no surprises at the end.

04

Post-Processing & Delivery

Final colour grading, retouching, and output in whatever format you need — print-ready, web-optimized, or video. Delivered on time, every time.
